Privacy Policy - KKM Vegetable

Last updated: June 29, 2026

KKM Vegetable ("the App") is a daily vegetable purchase management application used internally by KKM branches and administrators. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information submitted by users:

  • Branch names and login PINs
  • Daily vegetable order details (item names and quantities)
  • Purchase prices and expense records entered by administrators

The App does not collect personal contact information such as names, phone numbers, or email addresses from branch users.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Record and manage daily vegetable orders across branches
  • Calculate purchase costs and generate billing summaries
  • Maintain historical purchase records for business operations

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Firebase Realtime Database, a service provided by Google. Data is accessible only to authorized branch and admin users via PIN-based login.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share collected data with third parties. Data is used exclusively for internal business operations of KKM.

5. Data Security

Access to the App is restricted through PIN-based authentication. Administrators have additional access controls to manage sensitive pricing and billing information.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for internal business use by adult staff members and is not directed at children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app administrator.
